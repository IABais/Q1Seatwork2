Characters of Noli me Tangere

Image of the Character Name of the Character Description of the Character
Maria Clara She is the female lead of the novel and is the fiance of Ibarra. She is the supposed daughter of Capitan Tiago and Doña Pia Alba but her true father is actually Father Damaso after a scandalous event happened between him and Doña Alba.
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin Our main character of the story better known as "Ibarra". Is the son of Don Rafael Ibarra, he is a Mestizo who went to Europe to study for 7 years. And he has returned to the Philippines.
Father Damaso He is our main antagonist of the story. He is a arrogant priest of San Diego which he was resigned and move to another province.
Capitan Tiago He is a Filipino native-born and is also very wealthy. He is good friends with Don Rafael and Father Damaso, He is a Filipino who feels he is a Spaniard and is one of the richest people in his home town Binundok and he owns land in Laguna, Pampanga, and San Diego.
Don Rafael Ibarra The father of our protagonist, Don Rafael was the richest man in San Diego. But he was accused of being the enemy of the church and spanish government and was imprisoned and died due to a illness and weak body.
